Spin Up Millburn Results

At New Jersey's first regional competition at Millburn Saturday Showdown, 765N and 2616W won the tournament, beating out the double Millburn teams 7405N and 7405K. 2616W ended up winning the Skills Champion award with their catapult, and 765N used their flywheel to help score discs. 765X allianced with 4610V and lost in the semifinals to double Millburn. 765A qualified for the elimination round, choosing 4610E as their alliance partner after declining 4610X's alliance offer. Unfortunately, they lost in the round of 16 to 30A and 30B. Everyone had a great time at Millburn's Saturday Showdown, and we are looking forward to the next regional competition!

Tipping Point Team Results

High Tech's VEX teams have historically been high-performing in New Jersey and nationally. During the 2021-2022 Tipping Point season, all three teams – 765A, 765X, and 765N – qualified for the state championship tournament, with 765X triple qualifying. 765A and 765X then placed as Tournament Finalists during the state competition, earning them both a trip to Dallas, TX for the VEX Robotics World Championship in May. There, 765X placed near the top of their division during qualifiers, allowing them to compete in the division elimination matches and earn Division Finalist status at Worlds. This put them among the top 40 VRC High School teams at Worlds (which hosted more than 800 high school teams), in terms of head-to-head match placements.

The teams are looking forward to another great season with Spin Up!
