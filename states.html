New Jersey State Championship

765 is proud to co-host!

For the second year in a row – both in 2022 and 2023 – the FuryTech organization will co-host the New Jersey VRC State Championship at Bell Works in Holmdel, New Jersey. Along with our event partners Robot Revolution, we'll welcome about 50 top teams from across the state to compete for eight coveted VEX Worlds qualification spots. The 2022 event was a huge success, with great teams qualifying for Worlds, including 2 FuryTech teams and 2 Robot Revolution teams.

This season's event will be held on March 4, 2023.
